About The Festival

The Point Roberts Film Festival is a celebration of cinematic storytelling, showcasing the work of emerging filmmakers from our community and beyond. Our mission is to foster creativity and provide a platform for new voices in film.

This year's festival will feature special screenings of student films created during our workshop series, along with curated selections from independent filmmakers worldwide.

Film Submissions

We're excited to review your work for inclusion in the Point Roberts Film Festival. Our programming team carefully considers all submissions for both competitive and non-competitive screenings.

Submission Categories

  • Student Films (created in our workshops)
  • Short Films (under 30 minutes)
  • Feature Films (over 45 minutes)
  • Documentaries
  • Experimental Films and Music Videos
